Definiton

A movie, also referred to as a film or motion picture, is a captivating form of visual art that transports audiences into diverse worlds, narratives, and emotions through the medium of moving images. Accompanied by sound and occasionally other sensory elements, movies offer a rich tapestry of experiences, from heart-pounding action to heartfelt romance.
